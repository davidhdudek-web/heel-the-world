The Designer

David H. Dudek

Nobody handed me the shoemaker’s craft. I went and took it — through the women’s shoe manufactories of Eastern Europe, bench by bench, until I understood how a high heel truly comes to be.

Then the big houses called: Printemps, KaDeWe, Alsterhaus, Oberpollinger, BMW, CUPRA. I staged their brands and learned how an entrance is built.

Both go into every pair: the craft from Eastern Europe, the eye from Paris. Designed by me. For you.

Before we begin

Finding your fit.

Every pair is built around your foot, not the other way around. Two ways to get us there — choose the one that suits you.

Option 01
Precision Cast
A full 3D impression of your foot, taken at home with an alginate casting kit — the same material used in prosthetics and film. Every contour, every arch, captured exactly. The most accurate foundation we can build a shoe on, especially for unusual foot shapes or very high heels.
  • We send you a casting kit with full instructions
  • Mix the alginate, submerge your foot, wait a few minutes
  • Send the finished cast back to us
  • We build your last directly from the impression
Recommended for the most precise fit. Additional cost — ask us for current pricing.
Option 02
Standard Measurement
The classic method — the same principle standard sizing has always used, just done properly and sent directly to us. No kit to wait for, no mixing, done in five minutes with paper and a tape measure.
  • Measure in the evening — feet are at their largest then
  • Stand on a sheet of paper, weight even on both feet, and trace the outline
  • Mark the longest point (heel to longest toe) and the widest point
  • Wrap a tape measure around the ball of your foot for the circumference
  • Repeat for both feet — most people have a small difference between them
  • Photograph the tracing next to the tape measure and send both with your inquiry
Included at no extra cost. The right choice for most commissions.
